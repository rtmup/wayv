Healthcare redefined

Improving your total health – nutrition, fitness and mental wellbeing – should never feel like a chore, guessing game or lonely endeavor. With Wayv Health, it never will. We combine cutting-edge medical assessments, fitness recommendations and small group workouts to help you optimize your health.

How it works

  1. Get a total health assessment

    We build a comprehensive health profile to assess your physical fitness, nutrition and mental wellbeing.

  2. Receive a personalized plan

    Our experienced healthcare professionals create an easy-to-follow plan to help you improve your total health.

  3. Put your plan into practice

    Work out and build friendships with fellow Wayv members. Fellowship is the glue that holds a healthy life together. All training groups are limited to 15 people to ensure that fellowship is possible.

  4. Easily keep track of progress

    Track your physical fitness, nutrition and mental wellbeing online and get alerted if we spot health risks.
